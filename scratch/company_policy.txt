Company Policy Document

1. Refund Policy
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 7 days of purchase.
After 7 days, refunds are not permitted under any circumstances.

2. Return Policy
Products can be returned within 10 days if unused and in original packaging.

3. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves per year.
Unused leaves cannot be carried forward to the next year.

4. Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.

5. Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

6. Data Security
All employees must use company-approved devices and secure VPN connections.

7. Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professionalism and respect in all interactions.
